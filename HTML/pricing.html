Frequently asked questions

General

What's the difference between Basic,Plus, and Pro?
  • The Basic plan gives you access to free courses and the ability to start some more advanced courses.
  • Both Plus and Pro include everything in Basic, plus extra content and features like real-world projects, guided paths, and quizzes and practice to test your skills as you learn.
  • Plus is for people looking to learn or advance a skill — so it includes all courses and most skill paths.
  • Pro is for those looking to start or advance their career. In addition to all the features from Basic and Plus, Pro includes career paths, interview prep skill paths, code challenges, and career services.
  • Pro learners can also earn professional certifications in select career paths. To earn a professional certification, you need to pass a series of exams. Exams consist of multiple choice and coding questions.
  • Professional certifications are currently available for Full-Stack Engineer, Front-End Engineer, Back-End Engineer, Data Scientist: Machine Learning, Data Scientist: Analytics, and Computer Science.

Do I need to know how to code before signing up?

No, most of our users have zero experience when they sign up. Our curriculum is designed to teach anyone to learn to code.

Do you offer a student discount?

Yes! Eligible college students can join PlusAcademy Pro Student. It's an annual membership with all the same features as Codecademy Pro, but for over 35% off the regular price. Click here to join PlusAcademy Pro Student.

Upgrades

Why should I upgrade from Basics?

We built PlusAcademy Plus and Pro based on the lessons we've learned from teaching over 45 million people to code since 2011. If you're ready to accelerate your learning and apply your skills to real-world scenarios, either the PlusAcademy Plus or Pro membership is for you.

How do I upgrade from Plus to Pro?

If you're a current Plus member and want to update to Pro, you can upgrade in the payment and plan section of your account settings.

Canceling

How do I cancel my subscription?
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time in your payment settings. When you cancel, your subscription ends at the end of the current billing cycle.
  • You can also pause your subscription if you need to take a break.
  • Learn more in our Terms of Service.
